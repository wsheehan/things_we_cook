WHATS A RECIPE WITHOUT ITS STORY? THIS COOKBOOK TELLS THE STORY OF EACH RECIPE AND THE WONDERFUL COOKS WHO INSPIRED ME. THE THINGS WE COOK SHARES 200 OF MY MOST LOVED, TRIED AND TRUE RECIPES FROM THE KITCHEN OF GREEN HOPE FARM, OUR ORGANIC FARM NESTLED IN THE HILLS OF NEW HAMPSHIRE.

FOR THIRTY YEARS I HAVE COOKED UP A STORM FOR FAMILY, FRIENDS AND OUR BELOVED FARM STAFF.THESE STORIES AND RECIPES ARE ELEVATED TO A THING OF BEAUTY BY THE MARVELOUS WHIMSICAL ILLUSTRATIONS OF AllI HOWE, ONE OF THE MANY BELOVEDS WHO WORKED HERE AND FEASTED AT OUR FARM TABLE.

THE DIVERSE COMMUNITY SEATED AROUND OUR KITCHEN TABLE HAS ENCOURAGED ME TO EXPLORE DIFFERENT WAYS TO EAT AND COOK. HERE I SHARE ALL I HAVE LEARNED COOKING FOR THIS CROWD WITH THE FRUITS, VEGETABLES, MAPLE SYRUP AND HONEY FOUND AT OUR FARM. THERE ARE VEGAN RECIPES. THERE ARE GLUTEN FREE, DAIRY FREE, CORN FREE AND SUGAR FREE RECIPES. THERE ARE RECIPES FOR POTLUCKS, WINTER SUPPERS AND BIG CELEBRATIONS! THERE ARE COMFORT RECIPES CONTAINING MEAT AND DAIRY. NO MATTER WHO YOU COOK FOR, THE THINGS WE COOK OFFERS WONDERFUL RECIPES TO FEED YOUR BELOVEDS DELICIOUS FOOD!
